Privacy Policy

Socials Scheduler · Last updated 6 July 2026

Socials Scheduler is a personal, private software tool operated solely by its owner. It is used exclusively to schedule and publish content to social media accounts owned and managed by the operator.

Information we collect

Socials Scheduler does not collect, store, process, or share any personal information from any third party. The tool is not offered to the public and has no users other than its operator.

Third-party platforms

The tool connects to social media platforms (including TikTok) via their official APIs, solely to publish content to the operator's own accounts. Authentication credentials are stored locally on the operator's own device and are never transmitted to any party other than the relevant platform.

Data sharing

No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to any third party.
